About Christine; the artist behind every stitch.

Christine is very passionate about her creations. She's a hardworking, loving wife and mother of two amazing children who serve as the ultimate inspiration for those creations. She loves all things sparkly, and going out to buy yarn always makes her extremely happy. Most of her creations are brought to life in her home office where she works, mostly at night, as an online interpreter.
