About the artist behind every stitch:

Christine creations are all hand-crafted; her personal touch is what makes every creation stand out. Every product is intricately made and carefully assembled to suit every client's personal needs and preferences, and she always uses the best quality materials which yields the highest quality products. Christine devotes herself to -and really also prides herself on- delivering high quality crocheted and knitted products with love in every stitch. Christine is very passionate about her creations. She's a hardworking, loving wife and mother of two amazing children who serve as the ultimate inspiration for those creations. She loves all things sparkly, and going out to buy yarn always makes her extremely happy. Most of her creations are brought to life in her home office where she works, mostly at night, as an online interpreter.
